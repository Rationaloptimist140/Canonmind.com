00 · Executive Axiom

The Single Sentence
from Which Everything Follows.

CanonMind is not a media platform. It is an active cognitive filtering engine designed to preserve, route, and deliver timeless human signal — and to transform passive consumption into structured intellectual inquiry — in an era defined by the industrialised production of low-signal content.

02 · The Core Function

What CanonMind
Actually Is.

✗ It is not
A Media Catalog
A catalog is passive. IMDB is a catalog. The absence of judgment is not neutrality — it is a choice to treat noise and signal as equivalent. CanonMind refuses this equivalence as its founding act.
✗ It is not
A Recommendation Engine
Netflix, Spotify, and YouTube are recommendation engines. They optimise for watch time, not enrichment. Goodhart's Law applies: when a measure becomes a target, it ceases to be a good measure.
✓ It is
An Active Cognitive
Filtering Engine
Pre-filters at source · Contextualises at delivery · Filters supplementary context. Three operations no existing platform performs simultaneously.
The Three Operations

1. Pre-filters at the source. The KB is quality-gated before any user makes a request. There is no browsing through noise. The library is entirely composed of high-signal works. The decision about what to recommend has already been made — correctly — before the user arrives.

2. Contextualises at delivery. The CCF transforms every delivery from passive experience into structured inquiry. The user is not told what to think about Stalker. They are given a sequence of formal observations and a hypothesis to test against their own attention.

3. Filters supplementary context. The Exegesis Gate ensures that the interpretive layer around the primary work is as rigorous as the work itself. Not a surface-level YouTube explainer. Geoff Dyer — the man who wrote the definitive book on Stalker.

Rule 06 — Protecting High-Cognitive-Load Assets

Some works — Stalker, The Mirror, Andrei Rublev, Persona — achieve formal and philosophical heights that require preparation to access. This is not a failure of accessibility. It is a property of the work. Complex explanations require prepared receivers.

A user who encounters Stalker without having first completed Path 05 is not accessing Stalker — they are accessing a 161-minute film they cannot yet read. The experience is not merely diminished; it actively inoculates them against future engagement by producing an impression of incomprehensibility.
Rule 06 Advisory · Permanent CanonGuardian Amendment

Prerequisite routing is not gatekeeping. It is the recognition that demanding works reward the prepared receiver with a depth of experience that accessible works cannot provide. Rule 06 builds the infrastructure before routing. The specialist routing notation (16+, on-ramp required, CCF mandatory) is the system's explicit acknowledgment that these works are not difficult — they are demanding, which is different.

I · Critical Rationalism and the Explanatory Power Criterion
David Deutsch · Karl Popper · The Hard-to-Vary Criterion

Deutsch's most important insight — drawn from Popper's Critical Rationalism — is that the mark of a good explanation is that it is hard to vary. A good explanation explains much and cannot be casually adjusted without losing its explanatory power.

Applied to cultural objects, this criterion generates CanonMind's entire architecture.

The Metamorphosis at 9.9 — the highest score in the KB — is a perfect demonstration. Kafka's 80 pages cannot be reduced. Every sentence is doing structural work. The three-part structure IS the explanation of how capitalism converts persons into problems. Nothing can be varied without losing the explanation.
Canon Score 9.9 · The Metamorphosis · Kafka · 1915 · Phase 05 Batch 004

The Canon Score formula is a proxy for the Explanatory Power Criterion. Works that score highly do so because they are formally and philosophically hard to vary.

II · The Transmission Imperative (Joscha Bach)
Culture as Information Optimised for Cross-Mind Transmission

Bach's computational theory implies that culture is information optimised for transmission across the boundaries of individual minds and across time. Great works persist not because of institutional authority but because they encode genuine truths about human experience in formal structures that survive translation across minds, languages, eras, and contexts.

The Odyssey has been taught for 2,700 years because the structure it encodes — the homecoming, the faithfulness, the refusal of easier alternatives — maps onto something genuinely constant in human life. Remove the Greek context, the gods, the monsters. The core argument about longing and return remains intact.

CanonMind's 2050 test operationalises this. The Timelessness ×2 weighting is the system's formal commitment to transmission as the primary quality criterion. The CCF is a transmission mechanism: it converts the formal structure of the work into explicit propositions — transferable understanding rather than untransferable impression.

III · Why Rigorous Minds Converge on This Design

This is not accidental. The CanonMind architecture is the necessary consequence of applying intellectual rigour to the problem of cultural transmission. Rigorous minds share five properties that CanonMind directly addresses:

01
They value explicability
The Canon Score formula is explicit. Every integration verdict can be examined. The formula can be agreed with or contested. This is the opposite of 'trust the algorithm.'
02
They value falsifiability
The 2050 test is a falsifiable prediction. The CanonGuardian is the refutation mechanism. Every MONITOR verdict is a hypothesis under test.
03
They are impatient with low-signal environments
An intelligent person in a Netflix interface is a signal detector in a noise field. The Anti-Slop Gate removes the need for manual noise filtering. Intelligence is damaged, not served, by being forced to filter manually.
04
They understand that depth requires sequence
You cannot understand Stalker before Andrei Rublev. You cannot understand The Brothers Karamazov before Crime and Punishment. The prerequisite routing system mechanises the insight that knowledge accumulates and that sequence matters.
05
They want to be challenged, not entertained
Rigorous minds do not seek validation from media. They seek problems. Every high-scoring KB work is a formal and philosophical argument that demands engagement, testing, and a return visit.
IV · Against the Attention Economy — The Core Enemy
Goodhart's Law Applied to Culture

The attention economy rests on a single claim: that engaging content is valuable content. This claim is false. Engagement is a weak proxy for value. The human nervous system is reliably engaged by novelty, conflict, and social validation — none of which are correlated with the quality of the information triggering the engagement.

05 · System Constants

Seven Locked Rules.
Each a Logical
Consequence of the Ideology.

Every rule in the CanonMind system follows directly from the ideology. The rules are not administrative preferences — they are logical consequences of the core function.

CONSTANT 01
The Quality Gate is Binary
Works either clear ≥ 8.0 or they do not. No 'good enough' tier. From Critical Rationalism: a falsifiable claim is either confirmed or refuted. There is no partial confirmation.
CONSTANT 02
Timelessness is Double-Weighted
Because endurance is not a proxy for quality — it IS quality, for cultural objects. A formally brilliant work that will not survive the next generation has failed at the primary task of a great work.
CONSTANT 03
The 2050 Test is Explicit
Not 'will critics like this' — will this be discussed AND taught in 2050? The dual condition is deliberate: discussed without taught is noise; taught without discussed is cargo-culting. Both required.
CONSTANT 04
OOC Protocol Fires Without Exception
Any non-KB work triggers ⚠ OOC. Not a penalty — a formal acknowledgment that unverified works cannot route through verified infrastructure. The KB is the only verified ground.
CONSTANT 05
Maturity Routing is Never Modified by User Preference
The routing is not about restriction — it is about preparation. The routing serves the user's deeper interest, not their stated preference. A user requesting Stalker without Path 05 is being routed toward access, not away from it.
CONSTANT 06
Every Output Closes with Source Confirmation
Traceability is a requirement of any rigorous system. If a recommendation cannot be traced to a specific KB row, it has not been made by CanonMind — it has been improvised. Improvisation has no place in a quality-gated system.
CONSTANT 07
The Anti-Slop Gate Has No Exceptions
A poor interpretive frame, once loaded, is harder to dislodge than to prevent. The gate is the system's commitment to maintaining the quality of the entire interpretive ecosystem — not just the primary works themselves.
