The Canon Score

Quality, measured.
Not assumed.

Every work in the CanonMind library is scored against a six-dimension formula with Timelessness double-weighted. A work scoring below 8.0 never enters. A work whose Timelessness score is uncertain never enters.

CANON_SCORE = (Craft + Human Truth + Timelessness × 2 + Coherence + Educational + Accessibility) ÷ 7
Threshold ≥ 8.0 · Timelessness test: "Will this still be discussed and taught in 2050?" · Post-2010 = MONITOR (minimum 10 years)
01

Quality Gate

Every title must clear a minimum Canon Score before it's licensed. The library is intentionally small. The constraint is the product. We carry approximately 1% of what streaming platforms carry. We chose that 1% deliberately.

02

Cognitive Companion

Every film and album carries a Cognitive Companion File — timestamped semantic markers delivered alongside playback. Structural focus, cognitive insight, the 2050 verification, a watch instruction. Watching is studying.

03

Exegesis Filter

Every supplementary YouTube asset is scored against a six-dimension Exegesis Score (≥7.0 to pass). An Anti-Slop Gate blocks reaction videos, promotional content, and algorithmically optimised explainers before scoring even begins. No surface-level content.
